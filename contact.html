About One District One Product(ODOP)

The online ODOP scheme based system would be a software through which buyers can buy an unique product of any particular district.Here, local merchants of various districts can showcase their product and buyers can later buy those products through this platform.Products will be reviewed by various customers and top rated products will be honoured as the product of the year.This will enhance the quality of the products and will also increase the competitiveness among the local merchants.

  • Step One (ODOP Idea)

    Local Merchants of various districts need to give details about their products along with the product picture.

  • Step Two

    All the details of the form will be verified through this platform and after the correct verification, all that products will be showcased here.

  • Step Three

    After that,if any buyer wish to order any items, then they have to fillup all the necessary details. After which the request will be sent to the seller and they can directly communicate with the customers.

  • Step Fourth

    After the communications, the buyers can place an order which will be delivered to them by a delivery partner.
